<span style="font-size: 3rem; font-weight: bold; color: #1976d2;">Overview of The QUARKS Survey</span>

<div style="font-size: 1.2rem; line-height: 1.7; margin-top: 30px;">

The QUARKS survey (Querying Underlying mechanisms of massive star formation with ALMA-Resolved gas Kinematics and Structures) targets 139 dense protoclusters in massive star-forming regions, observed with ALMA Band 6 (λ ∼ 1.3 mm) at high angular resolution. With 156 pointings, QUARKS provides an unbiased sample to study massive star formation processes down to scales of ∼1000 au.

The survey aims to investigate fragmentation, protostellar outflows and disks, hub-filamentary accretion flows, and the physical and chemical evolution of dense cores, as well as feedback from young massive protostars.
