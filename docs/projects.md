# Projects

This section will collect follow-up science projects and related programs connected to ATOMS and QUARKS.

Possible project themes include:

- fragmentation and dense core demographics
- massive starless core candidates
- hub-filamentary accretion flows
- protostellar outflows and disk candidates
- chemical evolution in dense protoclusters
- feedback from young massive protostars
- multi-scale comparison between ATOMS Band 3 and QUARKS Band 6 products

The final project list can be organized by science theme, lead researcher, target subset, or publication status.
